Summary: The SAND website is now back online as an archive of our activities 2018-2020.


SAND went on hiatus in late 2020. Six years later, in 2026, we restored this website to archive the activities of SAND, 
our featured artists and collaborators. SAND is an independent curatorial project in Phoenix, Arizona. It was active 
from 2018 through 2020, during which we organized three exhibitions, published six editions of "SAND Mail" and penned 
one essay. 

For more information and to contact us, visit [the About page](https://sand.zone/pages/about.html).
